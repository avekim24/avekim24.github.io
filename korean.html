Simple Phrases IMPORTANT** You add a 요 at the end of a sentence when talking to a person older than you :

  • 나는 - I am

  • 너는 - You are

  • 안녕하세요 - Hello (formal, to an elder)

  • 안녕 - Hi (informal, to a friend or someone younger than you)

  • 어디있어요 - Where is

  • 저기있어요 - Over there

  • 네 - Yes

  • 아니요 - No

  • 감사합니다 - Thank you very much

  • 고맙습니다 - Thank you

  • 쥐송함니다 - I'm sorry

  • Bitte - Please

  • Sehr - Very

  • Gut - Good

  • Rechts - Right, on the right

  • Links - Left, on the left

  • Was - What

  • Und - And

  • Schön - Beautiful

  • Groß - Big, tall
